Basic stages:

  1. Low power movement, up to 100 watts (main target: FDM 3D printers)
  2. High-performance modules (main target: SLS 3D printers)
  3. Autonomus power management (accumulators controller, changers) -
  4. Autonomous robotics drones (main target: robots and drones)
  5. Medium power movement, up to 1000 watts (small power machines, low-power industrial robots)
  6. High power movement, up to 10000 watts (high-power machines, powerful industrial robots)
  7. Laser systems, up to 10000 watts - 16m
  8. Data processing (industrial systems for data processing ) -
  9. Data transmission (industrial systems for data transmission, wires and whireless)
